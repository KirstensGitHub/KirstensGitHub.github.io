Introduction

After graduating from the University of Southern California with a B.S. in Neuroscience, Kirsten transitioned to Dartmouth College where she has been serving as a lab manager in the Psychological and Brain Sciences Department.

Currently, she manages Professor Jeremy Manning's Contextual Dynamics Laboratory. Other research experience includes a year spent in Won Mok Shim's Perceptual Cognition Laboratory and time spent as an undergraduate research assistant at USC's Brain and Creativity Institute and UCLA's Cognitive Neurophysiology Laboratory.
