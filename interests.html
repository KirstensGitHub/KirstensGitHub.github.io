Interests

I study how people "control" what they consciously experience from moment to moment by shifting their attention. I specifically look at the ways this process influences what we store into (or experience from) our personal store of memories. I'm generally interested in consciousness, meta-cognition, dreams, and other far-out stuff (likeThe Cambridge Declaration of Consciousness (2012))

When I'm not sciencing, I like to do yoga, meditate, scribble in my dream journal..
